ValueSet: ParticipationMode

Official URL: http://terminology.hl7.org/ValueSet/v3-ParticipationMode Version: 0.1.0
Active as of 2014-03-26 Computable Name: ParticipationMode
Other Identifiers: OID:2.16.840.1.113883.1.11.16543

Identifies the primary means by which an Entity participates in an Act.
